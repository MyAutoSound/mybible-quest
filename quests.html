✦ Guided Quests

One long journey,
or a path for today.

Follow the main quest chapter by chapter, all the way from Genesis to Revelation — or pick up a short side quest built around exactly how you're feeling right now. Every quest pairs plain-language context with a short reflection at each step. Pick one up, put it down, come back whenever you like. Looking for something shorter still? Try a written prayer instead.

A note on approach

Not therapy, not a diagnosis

These quests are an educational, reflective way to sit with scripture alongside how you feel. They're not a substitute for professional support — if you're in crisis, please reach out to a counselor, doctor, or local support line.
