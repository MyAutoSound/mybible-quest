✦ Guided Quests

A path for however
you're feeling today.

Each quest is five verses long, with plain-language context and a short reflection prompt for every step. Pick one up, put it down, come back whenever you like — your progress is saved on this device.

A note on approach

Not therapy, not a diagnosis

These quests are an educational, reflective way to sit with scripture alongside how you feel. They're not a substitute for professional support — if you're in crisis, please reach out to a counselor, doctor, or local support line.
